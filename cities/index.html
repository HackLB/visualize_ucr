Violent Crime, absolute

This chart shows national and city violent crime numbers from 1975 to current, based on Uniform Crime Reporting data.

Choose a city from the list at the top right to filter data for just that city.

Click the headers at the top to toggle each category off or on.

** For years beginning 2015, this chart uses a revised definition of rape, per UCR changes. Therefore, comparisons across the 2014-2015 boundary must be understood in context of these changes.

*** National data shows only the vaggregate "Violent Crimes" summary.
